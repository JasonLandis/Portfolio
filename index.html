Constellation
April 2023 - August 2023
This 2D mobile arcade roguelike challenges you to build a constellation by exploring a procedurally generated universe, traveling between stars, and surviving waves of incoming asteroids coming from a direction of your choosing. Progress through increasingly difficult zones, unlock upgrades and star cosmetics, and adapt your strategy with every run.
This game was made in Unity.
This game is free to download on the App Store and the Google Play Store

GuessPhrase
August 2025 - January 2026
GuessPhrase is a daily word puzzle game inspired by Wordle, where players decipher a hidden phrase instead of a single word. Create an account to track your progress, add friends, and schedule custom phrases to be delivered to them on specific days throughout the year.
Visit the website at guessphrase.com
This web application was built using Angular, Express, and PostgreSQL.
